Curry Butter Chicken

Home

Description

A variation of curry made with marinated chicken and a spiced tomato and butter sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup full-fat yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp garam masala
  • 1 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 lbs bonesless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 inch knob of ginger, finely diced
  • Garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 Tbsp garam masala
  • 14 oz. crushed tomatoes
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • Steamed rice
  • Fresh cilantro

Directions

  1. Whisk together yogurt, garam masala, and salt in a medium bowl.
  2. Toss sliced chicken thighs in mixture until fully coated. cover and marinate for 30 minutes or overnight.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan until shimmering hot.
  4. Add chicken, without crowding the pan, and sear 2-3 minutes on each side. Once seared, set aside.
  5. Lower pan to medium heat. Add a splash more oil as well as your onions, ginger, sliced garlic, and salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Mix in your spices to the pan and let toast for 2 minutes.
  7. Add crushed tomatoes and water. Stir until incorporated over medium heat. Simmer until sauce has reduced (5-8 minutes).
  8. Add chicken back into the pan and simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  9. Add heavy cream and stir until incorporated. bring to a light simmer then reduce heat to low. Simmer for 3-4 minutes while stirring often.
  10. Remove from heat and stir in butter until incorporated. Season to taste with salt.
  11. Serve with steamed rice and topped with cilantro.
